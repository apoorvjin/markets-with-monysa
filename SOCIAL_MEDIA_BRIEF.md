## 2. The Product — Full Feature Inventory

The app has **5 tabs**: Markets, Trading, Investing, Macro, Profile. Below is every marketable feature with the concrete numbers that make good hooks.

### 2.1 Live Markets (Markets tab)
- **46 global stock indices**, **23 commodities**, **44 forex pairs** — live prices, interactive candlestick charts on tap.
- **Market-cap-weighted treemap heatmap** across **9 major indices**: S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, Dow Jones, Russell 2000, FTSE 100, DAX 40, Nikkei 225, Hang Seng, Nifty 50. Tile size = market cap (FX-normalized to USD so a Nikkei tile is comparable to an S&P tile), tile color = % change. Timeframes: 1D / 1W / 1M / YTD. *(Pro feature)*
- **CFTC COT data** — see what hedge funds are actually positioned in metals (gold, silver, etc.).
- Central bank policy rates around the world.
- Pre-market and post-market prices shown when the session calls for it.

### 2.2 AI Trading Signals (Trading tab)
- **BUY / HOLD / SELL signals with entry price, stop-loss, take-profit, and plain-English reasoning** for **49+ assets** (indices, commodities, forex, crypto).
- **9 strategies (S1–S9)** — S1–S3 free, all 9 on Pro. S9 is a specialist "Silver Liquidity Sweep" strategy for silver futures only (great niche-flex content).
- **Walk-forward backtests** for every asset and strategy: win rate, total return, max drawdown, Sharpe ratio, full trade log. The pitch: *verify the strategy before you trust the signal.*
- **Power Moves scanner** — a regime/breakout scanner across indices, forex, commodities, crypto with three Pine-script-aligned versions; signals like "thrust," "base," "new-high reclaim," "regime breakout."
- **Price alerts** with push notifications (3 free, unlimited on Pro).
- Watchlist, live quotes refreshing every 30 seconds, full-text stock search across global exchanges.
- **AI analyst notes** per asset (3/day free, unlimited Pro).

### 2.3 Investing tab (7 sections)
- **Tariff Exposure** — US tariff impact across **113+ countries**, each with an overall rate, per-sector breakdown, a 0–100 **impact score**, debt-to-USA exposure, and a plain-English explanation. Sortable by market size, rate, or name. **Free.** *(This is the app's most unique feature — nobody else has a consumer tariff browser.)*
- **Best Setups dashboard** — highest-probability setups filtered by backtested win rate, grouped by sector into "Leading" and "Improving." *(Pro)*
- **Multibaggers** — 10X-potential stock screener across **7 markets**: 🇺🇸 US, 🇮🇳 India, 🇬🇧 UK, 🇯🇵 Japan, 🇭🇰 Hong Kong, 🇨🇳 China, 🇪🇺 Euronext. Country-aware search: type any company, get it scanned.
- **Presidential trades** — transactions ≥ $100K parsed from official **OGE Form 278-T government ethics filings** (actual PDF disclosures, machine-read). Purchase/sale/exchange, date, amount range.
- **Smart Money** — two live strategies:
  - **Lobbying Growth**: companies with the fastest quarter-over-quarter growth in lobbying spend (source: US Senate lobbying-disclosure database).
  - **Insider Buys**: companies with the most insider buying in the last 90 days (source: SEC EDGAR).
- **Earnings Calendar** — upcoming earnings with symbol, sector, date.
- **ETF Explorer** — **42 curated ETFs across 7 categories** (sector, broad market, international, fixed income, commodity, thematic, leveraged/inverse). Holdings, sector weights, expense ratio, AUM, plus an **RRG rotation view** (Leading / Improving / Weakening / Lagging quadrants). **Free.**

### 2.4 Macro tab (5 sections)
- **Market Stress Meter** — one dial for overall market stress.
- **VIX gauge** and **CNN Fear & Greed Index** gauge.
- **Yield curve** with status (Normal / Flat / Inverted) plus a full year of 3-month/5-year/10-year/30-year yield history — the recession-signal chart.
- **Sector rotation (RRG)** — which of the 11 sectors are Leading, Improving, Weakening, Lagging vs the S&P.
- **Correlation matrix** across assets.
- **Crisis Playbook** — a curated history of past market crises: what happened, what worked. Evergreen content goldmine.
- **US Debt Clock** — live total US debt, daily increase, debt-to-GDP, fiscal-YTD deficit/spending/interest — sourced live from **US Treasury and World Bank APIs**.
- **Economic Calendar** — high-impact USD events (FOMC, CPI, NFP).
- **AI Macro Briefing** — one-tap AI-written analysis of current market stress. *(Pro)*

### 2.5 Asset Detail (any symbol)
Tap any asset anywhere → 5 tabs: Chart (TradingView or native candlesticks), Signal, Indicators/fundamentals, Backtest, News with sentiment scores. Works for essentially any Yahoo Finance ticker on Earth.

## 4. Positioning & Messaging

### Core value props (ranked for content mileage)
1. **"See what the smart money sees."** Presidential filings, insider buys, lobbying money, hedge-fund COT positioning — all in one consumer app.
2. **"Signals you can audit."** Every AI signal comes with entry/SL/TP *and* a walk-forward backtest. No black box, no guru screenshots.
3. **"The whole world's markets, not just the S&P."** 46 indices, 44 forex pairs, 9-index heatmap, 7-country stock screening.
4. **"Macro, decoded."** Stress meter, fear & greed, yield curve, crisis playbook — the stuff pros watch, explained plainly.
5. **"Tariffs, mapped."** The only app with a 113-country tariff impact browser. Own this niche.
6. **"Real data or a dash."** The honesty angle — live government/exchange sources (US Treasury, World Bank, SEC EDGAR, Senate LDA, CFTC, OGE), never fabricated filler.

### Competitive framing (imply, don't name-drop aggressively)
- vs. **brokerage apps**: they show you prices; FinBrio shows you *positioning, policy, and risk*.
- vs. **Bloomberg Terminal**: 1/2000th the price, fits in a pocket.
- vs. **signal-seller Telegram groups / finfluencers**: FinBrio backtests are on-device and transparent; no one is DMing you a "VIP group."

### Words to use
signals, setups, macro, positioning, exposure, regime, backtest, smart money, stress, rotation, playbook, edge (sparingly).

### Words/claims to NEVER use
- ❌ "Guaranteed returns," "can't lose," "get rich," specific profit promises.
- ❌ "Financial advice" — the opposite: **every piece of content that shows a signal, backtest stat, or stock name must carry a disclaimer** (see §7).
- ❌ "Congress trades tracking" — this feature was **removed** (data sources died). Presidential (OGE) and Smart Money (lobbying/insider) are the live features; don't advertise congressional stock tracking.
- ❌ "Real-time tick data" — quotes are live but refresh on intervals (30s for trading quotes). Say "live," not "real-time tick-by-tick."

## 5. Content Pillars & Formats

### Pillar A — "Data Drop" (educational, 40% of content)
Use the app's own data as the content. Screenshot or recreate a chart, add one sharp insight.
- *Twitter*: single chart + 2-line take. "The 3m/10y spread just went [X]. Last 4 times this happened: [crisis playbook data]."
- *Instagram*: carousel — slide 1 hook stat, slides 2–5 the breakdown, slide 6 app CTA.
- Recurring series ideas:
  - **"Stress Check Sunday"** — weekly Market Stress Meter + Fear & Greed reading.
  - **"Rotation Report"** — which sectors moved RRG quadrants this week.
  - **"The Debt Ticker"** — US debt grew $X since last post (the daily-increase number is live in the app).
  - **"Insider Radar"** — top insider-buy names this week (from the app's Smart Money tab).
  - **"Tariff of the Week"** — one country's tariff exposure card, explained.

### Pillar B — "Smart Money Watch" (curiosity/virality, 25%)
Presidential disclosures, lobbying spikes, insider clusters, hedge-fund metals positioning.
- Hooks: "A government official just disclosed a $100K+ purchase of ___." / "This company's lobbying spend grew 42% in one quarter. Here's what usually comes next."
- These are public filings — factual, sourced, inherently shareable. Always cite the source type (OGE filing, SEC EDGAR, Senate LDA).

### Pillar C — "Receipts" (product proof, 20%)
- Backtest breakdowns: "We backtested strategy S2 on gold over 5 years. Win rate: [screenshot]. Here's the full trade log — including the losers." Showing losing trades is *on brand* and disarms skepticism.
- Feature demos: 15–30s screen-record Reels — treemap zoom, tapping a signal, pulling up a country's tariff card.

### Pillar D — Skits & Relatable Humor (30-60s Reels/TikTok-style, 15%)
Persona-driven, low production. See §6 for scripts.

## 7. Compliance — NON-NEGOTIABLE

Every post that shows a signal, a ticker, a backtest stat, or anything resembling a trade idea must include (caption or end-card):

> *Informational only — not financial advice. Do your own research. Past performance does not guarantee future results.*

Additional rules:
- Never post a signal as a recommendation ("buy this now"). Frame as *"the app's S2 strategy currently reads BUY on ___"* — reporting what the tool shows.
- Presidential/insider/lobbying content: stick strictly to what the public filing says (name, date, amount range, type). No accusations of wrongdoing, no insinuations about individuals — "here's the public record" framing only.
- Backtests: always describe as historical/hypothetical. Show losers alongside winners when feasible.
- Data is from third-party providers (Yahoo Finance, US Treasury, World Bank, SEC EDGAR, Senate LDA, CFTC, OGE) and may lag or be unavailable outside market hours — don't screenshot-and-promise availability.
- App Store-safe language only in bio/ads: "Free to start. Pro and Insight tiers unlock advanced strategies, unlimited alerts, deeper backtests, and AI-driven analysis."

## 8. Platform Playbook

- **Cadence**: 1–2 posts/day. Mix: ~50% Data Drop one-liners with a chart, ~25% Smart Money Watch, ~15% threads, ~10% product.
- **Threads**: tariff country rankings, crisis playbook retrospectives, "how to read the yield curve" — end every thread with one app screenshot + link.

- **Voice**: dry, precise, numbers-first. Punchlines allowed; emojis sparingly (📊 📉 🏛️ fit the brand; no 🚀💎🙌 meme-stock energy).

### Instagram
- **Cadence**: 3–4 Reels/week, 1–2 carousels/week, daily stories.
- **Reels**: skits (§6) + 15s screen-capture demos. Always dark-mode UI, always end on the treemap or a gauge (most visual screens).

- **Stories**: daily "Stress Check" (stress meter + F&G screenshot), polls ("Is the market overheated? Vote"), countdowns to FOMC/CPI using the app's calendar.
- **Bio**: "Markets, signals & macro intel 📊 Free on iOS & Android" + link.
