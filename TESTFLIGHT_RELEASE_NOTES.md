# FinBrio — TestFlight Release Notes
_Covers changes from 2026-07-02 to 2026-07-06_

Copy-paste ready text for App Store Connect → TestFlight → "What to Test" below the divider.

---

We're now **FinBrio**! (formerly Monysa/Moby) — new name, new icon, same tools.

⚠️ **You'll need to sign in.** A free account is now required to use the app — sign in with Google or email, then verify your email address. Your watchlist, alerts, and plan will follow your account going forward. Apple Sign-In is visible but shown as "Coming Soon" — use Google or email for now.

**New**
• Sign in with Google, or with email — a redesigned welcome screen shows what FinBrio does (investing, trading, macro intel, heatmaps, built-in strategies) before you sign in.
• ETF Explorer (Investing tab) — browse 42 curated ETFs across 7 categories (Sector, Broad Market, International, Fixed Income, Commodity, Thematic, Leveraged) with holdings, sector weights, expense ratio, and AUM per fund, plus a rotation (RRG) view. Free, no plan required.
• Advanced Correlation (Macro tab) — strongest-pair highlights, rolling correlation history charts, and the ability to track your own custom symbols.
• Signal Compare (Trading → Signals, Pro) — compare a strategy's base signal (S1–S9) against its enhanced version (S1+–S9+) side by side.
• Native in-app candlestick charts — faster charts with synced-zoom indicator panes (RSI/MACD/Stoch/ATR/ADX) and a log-scale toggle.
• Push notifications — opt in from Profile to get alerted when VIX flips between contango and backwardation.
• 10X Backtest now shows a full results breakdown table, not just headline stats.

**Improved**
• Verification emails now arrive branded from FinBrio directly, with a reminder to check spam/junk if one doesn't show up.
• Live US Debt Clock — debt, deficit, spending, and interest now pull from real Treasury/World Bank data instead of placeholders; stats we couldn't source live (debt-per-taxpayer, SS/Medicare unfunded liabilities) were removed rather than left stale.
• Smart Money (Lobbying Growth + Insider Buys) now matches company names to tickers far more reliably.
• Tariff Exposure country list shows an at-a-glance Impact score badge next to each rate.
• Profile settings (theme, notifications, chart provider, font size) consolidated into one Settings card.
• Refreshed app icon and logo now appear consistently across the sign-in screen, splash screen, and every tab's header.

**Removed**
• Congress Trades and House Trades tabs — both underlying data providers stopped returning usable data, so they were removed rather than shown broken. Smart $ (Lobbying + Insider Buys) is unaffected — different, confirmed-live sources.
